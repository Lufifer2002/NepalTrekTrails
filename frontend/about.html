About Nepal Trek Trails

Your trusted partner for unforgettable Himalayan adventures

Our Story

Founded with a passion for adventure and a deep love for the Himalayas, Nepal Trek Trails has been guiding travelers through Nepal's most spectacular landscapes for over 15 years. What started as a small local operation has grown into one of Nepal's most trusted trekking companies.

Our journey began when our founders, experienced mountaineers and local guides, recognized the need for authentic, safe, and sustainable trekking experiences. They combined their extensive knowledge of the mountains with a commitment to responsible tourism, creating a company that truly cares about both our clients and the communities we visit.

Today, we continue to uphold these values while expanding our offerings to include a wide range of trekking routes, from beginner-friendly trails to challenging high-altitude adventures.

Our Mission

To provide safe, authentic, and transformative trekking experiences that connect travelers with the natural beauty and rich culture of Nepal. We are committed to:

  • Ensuring the highest safety standards for all our trekkers
  • Promoting sustainable and responsible tourism practices
  • Supporting local communities and preserving cultural heritage
  • Creating memorable experiences that inspire a love for adventure

Our Vision

To be the leading trekking company in Nepal, recognized for our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and authentic cultural experiences. We envision a future where tourism benefits both travelers and local communities while preserving the pristine beauty of the Himalayas for generations to come.
